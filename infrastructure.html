🏗️ Infrastructure in Rudain

Rudain village has made remarkable progress in infrastructure, blending tradition with modern amenities. Here are some key highlights:

🛣️ Paved Roads

Well-maintained concrete and asphalt roads connect different parts of the village, improving mobility and safety.

🔆 Solar Energy

Solar-powered street lights and water pumps promote energy efficiency and reduce dependency on the grid.

🚰 Clean Water & Sanitation

New pipelines and sanitation facilities ensure access to clean drinking water and hygienic living conditions.
